# OHM Icon Descriptions

Names and descriptions for each candidate logo in this folder.
The gallery page (index.html) fetches this file at load time —
edit here to update what appears on the preview site.

Format: each entry starts with `## filename.svg`, followed by
`name:` and `description:` lines.

---

## 01-hourglass-globe.svg
name: Hourglass Globe
description: An hourglass silhouette in deep teal with a golden globe at each bulb — time as two worlds, one past and one future, connected at the waist.

## 02-hourglass-water-land.svg
name: Hourglass Water & Land
description: The same hourglass form, but each globe shows blue oceans and a green landmass — pairing the classic time symbol with an explicit world map.

## 03-pyramid-tower.svg
name: Pyramid & Tower
description: A sandstone pyramid beside a dark medieval tower — two iconic built structures spanning ancient and medieval history in a single silhouette.

## 04-sundial.svg
name: Sundial
description: A semicircular dial face in deep burgundy with a triangular gnomon — one of humanity's oldest instruments for reading time from the landscape.

## 05-folded-map.svg
name: Folded Map
description: An accordion-folded paper map crossed by a horizontal timeline with arrows at each end — the tension between navigating space and navigating time.

## 06-simplification.svg
name: Simplification
description: A green folded map in the background with the OHM lettermark enlarged in a magnifier circle overlay — exploring how the brand reads at reduced complexity.

## 07-negative-space.svg
name: Negative Space
description: A dark map silhouette with a circular window cut from its center, revealing the OHM lettermark in the void — the map as field, the mark as figure.

## 08-map-hourglass-cutout.svg
name: Map · Hourglass Cutout
description: An hourglass shape punched through a dark folded map, revealing white behind it — time as an absence, a window cut through geography.

## 09-map-sundial-cutout.svg
name: Map · Sundial Cutout
description: A sundial arc and gnomon cut from a dark map as negative space — the ancient timepiece as a void in the cartographic form.

## 10-ohm-concept.svg
name: OHM Concept
description: The three OHM elements layered: a green map tile, a blue ocean globe, and a bold orange hourglass — a full-composition test of the combined symbols.

## 11-ohm-with-border.svg
name: OHM with Border
description: The same three-element composition as #10, but with the hourglass rendered as an orange outline on white — a more refined, bordered treatment.

## 13-square-monster.svg
name: Square Monster
description: The OHM lettermark built from thick, angular rectangular strokes on parchment — a bold stamp-like treatment with blocky, historical character.

## 14-curved-monster.svg
name: Curved Monster
description: The OHM lettermark redrawn as a single continuous rounded-stroke path — the same monogram energy as #13, but fluid and organic.

## 15-curved-monster-water.svg
name: Curved Monster · Water
description: The curved lettermark from #14 with a blue water fill rising through the lower half — geography and typographic form meeting in the mark.

## 16-hourglass-in-globe.svg
name: Hourglass in Globe
description: A golden hourglass held inside a deep blue globe circle — time contained within the world, or the world built around the passage of time.
