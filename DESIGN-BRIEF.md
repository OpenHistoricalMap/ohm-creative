## Project

[OpenHistoricalMap](https://www.openhistoricalmap.org) (OHM) is a free, open, wiki-style map of the world throughout history — think OpenStreetMap, but across time. We are seeking a new logo and favicon to replace the current mark.

## Current logo

![Current OHM logo](https://www.openhistoricalmap.org/assets/ohm_logo-884051b901193cf1d229cb08e81d60ab7b28aea9ba53acad8f141dca78c86430.svg)

## Goals

- Communicate the intersection of **maps** and **time**
- Work at small sizes (favicon: 16×16px) as well as large (header, print)
- Feel open, collaborative, and historically minded — not corporate
- Be clearly distinct from the OpenStreetMap logo while sharing its spirit
- Work in monochrome as well as color

## Constraints

- Must be delivered as SVG
- Should include a dedicated favicon variant optimized for 16×16px
- Colors should work on both light and dark backgrounds
